# B2B Outreach Scripts & Templates

This document contains the cold calling script flow and follow-up email templates for Northern Rivers Mat Hire. Use these scripts to contact local shopfronts and follow up with custom mat packages tailored to their business type.

---

## Part 1: Cold Call Script Flow
*Designed for Kerry or Matt to sound natural, helpful, and hyper-local.*

### 1. Introduction & Hook
> *"Hi there, is the owner or manager available? ... Hi, my name is [Kerry/Matt]. I'm calling from **Northern Rivers Mat Hire**, we’re a local family-owned business based right here in the area, and we help local businesses keep their floors clean, dry, and safe."*

### 2. The Local & Contract-Free Value Pitch
> *"The reason I'm reaching out is that many local storefronts are tired of being locked into rigid, multi-year contracts with big national corporate suppliers. We do things differently—we have zero lock-in contracts, and we offer a **Seasonal Adjustment Strategy** where you can easily scale your mat swaps weekly, fortnightly, or monthly depending on your foot traffic."*

### 3. The Offer Stack (Choose based on response or industry)
*   **The 14-Day Free Trial (Low friction):**
    > *"To show you the quality, we're offering local shops a **14-day free trial** of our premium charcoal entry mats. We drop a fresh mat off, do a swap after a week, and pick it up at the end. It's 100% free, zero obligation, just to let you test it out."*
*   **The First Month Half-Price (Value-driven):**
    > *"If you want to start a regular service, we're doing a launch promotion where your **first month is half-price**, with absolute freedom to cancel or change your frequency at any time."*
*   **The Custom Logo Mat Deal (Brand-driven - great for boutiques/cafes):**
    > *"We also do custom high-definition logo mats. Right now, we're offering **50% off the custom logo setup fee** when you sign up for a flexible 6-month rental agreement. It's a great way to welcome customers."*

### 4. Setting Up the Follow-up (The Close)
> *"I know you're busy running the shop right now. Would it be okay if I send a quick, personalized email with our sizes, pricing, and how the free trial works, so you have it on file? ... Great, what's the best email address for that?"*
