## 1. Paid Campaign Strategy (Local Business Targeting)
To generate high-quality B2B leads in the Ballina to Tweed Heads corridor, we will run highly targeted paid advertisements.

### 1.1 Audience Targeting Criteria
*   **Geographic**: Pin drop targeting on Byron Bay, Ballina, Tweed Heads, Lismore, Kingscliff, and Bangalow (+10-20km radius to cover commercial estates).
*   **Demographic / Job Titles**: 
    *   Interests: "Small business", "Cafe", "Retail", "Restaurant management", "Hospitality industry".
    *   Behaviors: "Facebook Page Admins", "Business Page Admins", "Retail Page Admins".
*   **Age**: 25 - 65+ years.

*   **Objective**: Lead Generation (using Facebook's Instant Forms).
