Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our mat hire schedules or purchases? Read our FAQs below or contact Kerry.

What towns and areas do you service?

We service the coastal corridor from Ballina in the south to Tweed Heads in the north, extending up to 50 km inland. This covers major towns including Byron Bay, Lismore, Murwillumbah, Bangalow, Mullumbimby, Kingscliff, and surrounding Northern Rivers suburbs.

How does the adjustable frequency scheduling work?

Unlike corporate contracts that trap you, we offer complete freedom. If business is quiet in winter, you can shift from a 1-week service to a 4-week schedule to save costs. When tourist season returns, you can dial it back to 2 weeks or 1 week. Just let us know and we will adjust your billing.

Can you supply custom sizes?

Yes, we can supply custom sizes to fit any location. We work with our supplier, The Mat Group, to create made-to-measure matting layouts (up to any length) for shops, revolving doors, long hallways, or commercial kitchens. Let us know your dimensions in the booking form.

What welcome promotions do you offer?

We offer two massive welcome promotions: 1. First Month Half-Price on all standard mat hire, with zero lock-in contracts. 2. 50% Off Custom Logo Mat Setup/Purchase when you sign a 6-month hire agreement. We apply these automatically to qualified configurations.

Are we locked into long-term contracts?

No! Our hire service offers a zero lock-in agreement, meaning you can cancel at any time. The only contract we offer is a simple, flexible 6-month agreement which you can select to unlock the 50% discount on your custom printed logo mats.
