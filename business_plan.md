# Business Plan - Northern Rivers Mat Hire
**Positioning & Strategic Playbook for the Northern Rivers Region**

---

## 1. Executive Summary
**Northern Rivers Mat Hire** is a specialized, locally-owned B2B facility service provider offering commercial floor mat rental, laundering, and outright sales. We service the coastal corridor from **Ballina to Tweed Heads**, extending **50 km inland** (including Byron Bay, Lismore, Murwillumbah, Bangalow, and Mullumbimby).

We have structured our business into two clear divisions:
1.  **Hire Mats (Scheduled Rental & Laundering)**: Exclusively offering the charcoal *Entry Plush Mat* on flexible exchange schedules.
2.  **Purchase Mats (Outright Buy)**: Offering a high-end catalog of commercial floor mats sourced from *The Mat Group Australia* at a retail markup, using custom branding and names to establish local B2B value.

---

## 2. Competitive Landscape & Niche Playbook
To win business over national giants (like Alsco and Initial Hygiene), we avoid rigid, multi-year contracts and focus on hyper-local responsiveness and commercial flexibility.

### 2.1 The "Seasonal Adjustment" Strategy (No Pauses Needed)
Instead of allowing businesses to pause their runs completely (which leaves mats unmaintained and risks losing the account), we offer **Flexible Frequency Tuning**. 
*   **How it works**: Businesses can scale their exchange frequency between **weekly**, **fortnightly**, and **monthly** at their leisure. 
*   **Strategic Benefit**: During slow winter seasons or tourism dips, a Byron Bay shop can easily shift from weekly swaps to monthly swaps. This keeps their floors protected and the mat clean, reduces their costs, and keeps the active service contract alive. When peak season returns, they scale back up to weekly.

### 2.2 Welcome Campaigns & Promotions
To lower the friction of B2B client acquisition, we offer two main introductory incentives:
1.  **First Month Half-Price (No Lock-In)**: New rental clients receive a 50% discount on their first month of service, with absolute freedom to cancel at any time. This destroys B2B onboarding skepticism.
2.  **50% Off Custom Logo Setup (6-Month Agreement)**: Customers who want their own branding printed on entry mats receive a 50% discount on the logo setup/purchase fee when they sign a standard 6-month rental agreement.

## 3. Product Offerings & Specifications

### 3.1 Hire Mats (Rental Service)
To maximize washing efficiency and inventory control, our hire service focuses on a single premium line:
*   **Product**: *Entry Plush Mat* (Classic heavy-duty nylon carpet top with a slip-resistant nitrile rubber gripper backing).
*   **Color**: Charcoal only (the gold standard for masking dirt and sand).
*   **Standard Sizes (The Mat Group specifications)**:
    *   `600 x 850 mm` (Small single door)
    *   `850 x 1500 mm` (Medium entryway - standard)
    *   `850 x 3000 mm` (Long hallway runner)
    *   `1150 x 1800 mm` (Wide double door entryway)
*   **Service Options**: Weekly, Fortnightly, or Monthly exchange.

### 3.2 Purchase Mats (Outright Buy)
For clients who do not need laundry service and prefer to own their mats, we resell a curated B2B range at a **20% retail markup** over *The Mat Group* wholesale pricing. 

Our catalog uses custom product names:
1.  **Coir Eco-Weave** (Natural organic coconut husk fibers for rustic/boho entrances). *Starts at $84.00*
2.  **UltraScrape Loop** (Textured vinyl loops for outdoor scraper duty). *Starts at $60.00*
3.  **Superguard Ribbed** (High-traffic double-ribbed entry matting). *Starts at $88.00*
4.  **Signature HD Brand** (High-definition custom logo printing). *Starts at $412.00* (Promoted: *50% off logo fee on 6-month hire contracts*)
5.  **ComfortStand Ergonomic** (Cushioned grease-resistant anti-fatigue matting). *Starts at $114.00*

*Note: Large-city specialized items (such as heavy-duty industrial assembly line rolls, cleanroom tacky mats, and architectural metal grids) are excluded to keep our local catalog highly relevant and simple.*

## 4. Financial Structure & Estimates

### 4.1 Rental Pricing Structure
We offer standard B2B rental estimates based on size and exchange frequency (user-customizable in `app.js`):
*   **600 x 850 mm**: Weekly ($8.50/wk) | Fortnightly ($10.20/wk) | Monthly ($12.75/wk)
*   **850 x 1500 mm**: Weekly ($12.50/wk) | Fortnightly ($15.00/wk) | Monthly ($18.75/wk)
*   **850 x 3000 mm**: Weekly ($20.00/wk) | Fortnightly ($24.00/wk) | Monthly ($30.00/wk)
*   **1150 x 1800 mm**: Weekly ($16.50/wk) | Fortnightly ($19.80/wk) | Monthly ($24.75/wk)

### 4.2 Outright Purchase Markup
All purchased mats are marked up 20% over supplier costs. Standard border options (such as heavy-duty bevelled rubber safety borders) are available, with bespoke quotes generated on demand for custom sizes up to any length.
